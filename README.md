      <h2>About Jadia (JNEW Artistry)</h2>
      <p>I’m Jadia N. E. Wilson — artist, educator, and founder of JNEW Artistry. Originally from Manchester, Jamaica, I hold an associate degree in visual art from Northern Caribbean University and work across painting, pen-and-ink portraiture, illustration, photography, and large-scale murals. My practice centers on color-forward storytelling, identity, and mental health awareness. I create bespoke commissions for murals, portraits, editorial illustration, and custom merchandise.</p>
